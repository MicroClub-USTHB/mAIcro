Hello Our Dear @IT members Finally the learning season is coming soon , the season that IT organize it yearly for its members to augment their knowledge about technical fields , sharp the old and gain new skills But There is No Learning Season without our dear @IT Trainers the people that kindly share their experience, ideas and of course knowledge about specific field and host workshops and bootcamps about it

How to become an IT Trainer ?

  • If you have specific training idea you want to host and share you knowledge about it with others, you can fill the form in the link below,describe your skills, choose the fields you want to host the workshop about, a topic if you have specific one and Finally your motivation to become an @IT Trainer
  • An important note to mention is that you can host any workshop about any topic even if it is basics of basics (HTML,CSS,specific programming language basics,...ect), because we believe that everyone has something to share and teach others regardless of the level of the topic

What are the fields that I can host a workshop about ?

  • Frontend Development: any topic related to web frontend development (HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React, Angular, Vue, Svelte, ...etc)
  • Backend Development: any topic related to web backend development (Node.js, Express, Django, Flask, Ruby on Rails, ...etc)
  • DevOps: any topic related to DevOps (Docker, Kubernetes, Jenkins, Version Control Systems, ...etc)
  • Artificial Intelligence: any topic related to AI (Machine Learning, Deep Learning, Computer Vision, Natural Language Processing, ...etc)
  • Mobile Development: any topic related to mobile development (Android, iOS, React Native, Flutter, ...etc)
  • Cybersecurity: any topic related to cybersecurity (Web Security, Network Security, Penetration Testing, Reverse Engineering, ...etc)

Note

  • you can host a workshop about any other field that is not mentioned above, you can just contact us and we will discuss about it

Why I should become an IT Trainer ?

بسم الله الرحمن الرحيم قال تعالى : "وَلْتَكُن مِّنكُمْ أُمَّةٌ يَدْعُونَ إِلَى الْخَير وَيَأْمُرُونَ بِالْمَعْرُوفِ وَيَنْهَوْنَ عَنِ المُنكَرِ ۚ وَأُوْلَئِكَ هُمُ الْمُفْلِحُونَ" - آل عمران آية 104
  • Sharing our knowledge to the next generations and the next generations will share it to the next generations and so on creating a chain of knowledge that will never end إن شاء الله, and what is better than صدقة جارية that will never end.
  • Hosting a workshop will help you to gain new skills, improve your communication skills, script writing skills, and of course your technical skills by preparing the workshop content (slides, code snippets, ...etc) and presenting it to the attendees.
  • Finally hosting a workshop (espiecally in advanced topics) will help you improve your understanding about the topic, the process of preparing the workshop content will force you to dive deep into the topic and understand it in a better way.
