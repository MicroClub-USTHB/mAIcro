Now it’s time for Integration System – Phase 2: the Welcome Week.

What Is It?

The Welcome Week is a series of workshops and talks organized by all sections and departments throughout one week to officially welcome our @New Weavers . Through this program, you will:
  • Discover what each entity does not just in theory, but through interactive workshops.
  • Learn how the club operates from the inside.
  • Interact with experienced members, understand their roles, and learn from their journey.
  • Identify which section or department fits you best.
By the end of the week, you’ll have a clear vision of how MC works and where you truly belong.

When and Where

  • Start date: November 12
  • Platform: Discord Server
